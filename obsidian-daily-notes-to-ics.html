Obsidian Daily Notes → iCalendar

Pick your Obsidian daily-notes folder, scan recursively for time-block tasks (completed and incomplete, including subfolders), then download a combined calendar file (.ics, .ical, .icalendar or .ifb). Indented bullets under a task become the event description. Optionally, use #tags or @labels on task lines to categorise events.
